Our Story

Built on Purpose, Grown Through People

Delcon is a multi-disciplinary contracting service active in both the public and private sectors throughout Southern Africa. Our company was born from a need to address a lack of economic development, high unemployment, and a shortage of skills in rural areas.

Starting from humble beginnings, we have grown into an innovative Black Economic Empowerment (BEE) company built on the principles of "Batho Pele" — People First. We are a Level-2 BBBEE compliant firm that prides itself on job creation and the advancement of South Africa.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a proud contributor to the development of Southern Africa by laying solid foundations for infrastructure and construction projects — including residential, community, and trenchless technology implementation.

Our Mission

To be the preeminent provider of superior construction services by consistently improving our quality. We aim to add value for clients through innovation and thorough performance, serving with character to bring honour to God.
